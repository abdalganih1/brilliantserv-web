# Strategic SEO & GEO Content Plan: "Dominate Riyadh 2026"

This plan outlines the strategy to generate 100+ high-ranking articles targeting the Saudi Arabia market (Riyadh, Jeddah, etc.) for pumps, mechanical, and electrical services. It leverages "Generative Engine Optimization" (GEO) to ensure visibility in AI answers (ChatGPT, Gemini) and traditional SEO supremacy.

## 1. Core Philosophy: The "Answer Engine" Approach
To rank #1 in Google and be cited by AI, every article must be the **definitive answer** to a specific query.
- **Structure**: Direct Answer (first 50 words) -> Detailed Explanation -> structured Data.
- **Entity Density**: Use specific brand names (KSB, Grundfos), part names (mechanical seal, bearings), and locations (Al-Malaz, North Riyadh) frequently.
- **GEO Tactics**: Use statistical data, tables, and "How-to" lists to increase citation probability.

## 4. Execution Plan for "Juels" (Automation)

**Phase 1: Database Creation**
1. Create `topics.json` containing 100+ rows.
   - Columns: `keyword`, `category`, `location`, `intent` (repair/supply/install).
   - Use the Python script to permute: {Service} x {Location} x {Modifier}.

**Phase 2: Image Generation/Assignment**
1. Map each article to a relevant image from `media/`.
2. Generate Alt Text dynamically: `"{Keyword} - روائع الكريستال للخدمات الهندسية"`.

**Phase 3: Content Generation**
1. Use the script to loop through `topics.json`.
2. Inject specific variables into the HTML template.
3. Randomize content slightly to avoid "Duplicate Content" penalties.

**Phase 4: Linking Strategy**
1. **Internal Linking**: Every article must link to:
   - Its "Hub" page (e.g., Services).
   - The "Contact" page.
   - 2 related articles in the same category.
2. **Update `llm.txt`**: Append all new URLs to `llm.txt`.
